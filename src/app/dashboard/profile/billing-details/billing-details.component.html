Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I upgrade?

When you upgrade, you will gain access to all premium features immediately.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings.

Is there a refund policy for plan upgrades?

Refunds are available within the first 7 days after upgrading your plan. If you’re eligible, contact support to process your refund.

Will my data be saved if I downgrade my plan?

Yes, your data will be saved if you downgrade, but some features might be disabled based on your new plan’s limitations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and certain international payment options. Please check the billing page for the latest information.

Will I be charged immediately when I upgrade?

Yes, upgrading triggers an immediate charge based on the chosen plan. The billing cycle resets, and your new plan's features are available instantly.

What happens at the end of my billing cycle?

At the end of your billing cycle, your subscription will automatically renew unless you choose to cancel or change your plan before the renewal date.

Is there a free trial available for premium plans?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users. During the trial, you’ll have access to all premium features, allowing you to explore the full potential of our service.

How can I change my billing information?

You can update your billing information by visiting the billing section in your account settings. All changes will apply from the next billing cycle.

What if I have more questions?

For further assistance, please contact our support team via email or chat. We’re here to help!
