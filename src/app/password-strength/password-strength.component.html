Password Strength Best Practices

  • Make it unique: Use passwords unique to different online accounts.
  • Make it random: Ensure your passwords do not follow a recognizable pattern.
  • Make it long: Use long passwords consisting of 14 characters or more.

Why does password strength matter?

Using the same password repeatedly across multiple sites might be convenient, but it also introduces a huge security risk. Choosing a unique and complex password for every online account helps ensure that your private information stays safe and secure.
