Roadmap of our application

Explore the development roadmap of SafePassVault. Stay updated on the features we are implementing to enhance your security and convenience.

Set up your own server, manage your data independently, and configure SafePassVault for self-hosting.

AI Integration

Enhance automation and user experience by integrating AI models for personalized insights and functionality.

Manage and edit files directly from the SafePassVault web interface for seamless control.

Boost your vault's security with biometric authentication, zero-knowledge encryption, and 2FA.

Settings & Customization

Personalize your vault with theme customization, export options, and notification settings.

Password Health Dashboard

Monitor and enhance your password security with AI-driven analysis and actionable insights.

Emergency Access

Assign trusted contacts to access your vault in emergencies, with customizable access conditions.

Activity Logs

Track all actions performed in your account, including logins, sharing events, and more.

Team Vaults

Collaborate securely by sharing a dedicated vault with your team members, with role-based permissions.

Breach Monitoring

Get notified if your credentials are part of a data breach and take immediate action to secure them.

Offline Mode

Access your vault without internet connectivity and sync updates automatically when online.

Backup & Restore

Securely back up your vault and restore it anytime to avoid losing critical data.

Voice Assistant Integration

Use voice commands to access your vault with integrations for Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant.
